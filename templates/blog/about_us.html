About US


Who we are

Asian American Leaders (AAL) association is a non-profit organization.

Our mission is to build and connect an Asian community for industry professional leaders for sharing information and resource.


What we do

We connect with existing industry leaders for sharing the successfully path for how they successfully become leader in a corporate. We also provide connection and resource for the young professional who are willing to become leaders and managers in future.

We create blogs and videos for sharing the successfully stories and learning materials on our website and the most social media platform.
